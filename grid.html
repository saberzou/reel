About

A NOTE

I'm Saber. I make things — products at Microsoft by day, doodles and photographs by night. This journal is where the films I see meet the visual rules I love: warm paper, ink, restraint, a single confident idea.

Every poster begins with a feeling I want to keep. Then I distill it down — fewer elements, more attention. The result isn't an advertisement. It's a souvenir.

Colophon
Set in Cormorant Garamond & Inter, with JetBrains Mono for marginalia. Designed and illustrated by Saber Zou. The visual register draws from Polish poster school, Criterion Collection, and the quiet confidence of a museum catalog. Posters are made with care, one film at a time.
